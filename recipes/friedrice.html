Fried Rice

Description

This is a simple fried rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Garlic
  • Thai chilli
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put oil in a hot pan and let it heat up
  2. Put in the garlic (peeled) and chilli (cut) for about 30 second
  3. Pour the rice in the pan and stir it until its homogenous
